# Ceremonies

## Design Review

- **When:** Before multi-agent work or any change that crosses content and backend boundaries.
- **Owner:** Ripley
- **Participants:** Relevant delivery agents, with Rai included when learner-facing content may carry safety risk.
- **Outcome:** Agreed scope, interfaces, owners, and review checkpoints.

## Retrospective

- **When:** After major milestones, major releases, or notable delivery failures.
- **Owner:** Ripley
- **Participants:** Everyone involved in the milestone.
- **Outcome:** What worked, what did not, and concrete follow-up actions for the next cycle.
