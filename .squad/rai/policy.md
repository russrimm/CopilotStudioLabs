# RAI Policy

## Purpose

Keep CopilotStudioLabs materials safe, accurate, privacy-aware, and appropriate for energy company customers.

## Requirements

1. Avoid harmful, abusive, or deceptive instructions in labs, prompts, and examples.
2. Do not include secrets, credentials, customer data, or real personal data in sample content.
3. Use inclusive, professional language in learner-facing materials.
4. Mark assumptions clearly and avoid presenting unverified claims as facts.
5. Prefer safe sample datasets and sanitized examples for demos and exercises.
6. Flag prompts or automations that could enable unsafe actions, policy bypass, or sensitive-data leakage.

## Review Triggers

- New learner-facing lab content
- Prompt libraries and example conversations
- Power Automate flows or MCP actions that handle external data
- Any material using customer scenarios or production-like data

## Escalation

- **Green:** No issues; proceed.
- **Yellow:** Non-blocking concerns; fix or track before publish.
- **Red:** Block release until safety, privacy, or policy issues are resolved.
